What is the Fox Process?

The Fox Process is a step-by-step methodology inspired by the adaptability and cleverness of foxes. It helps solve problems creatively and efficiently.

Steps in the Fox Process

  1. Observe: Study your surroundings and gather all necessary information.
  2. Adapt: Tailor your approach to the situation at hand.
  3. Act: Implement your plan with precision and agility.
